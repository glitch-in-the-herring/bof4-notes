* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC007.EMI: 00016278` in the files
	* `0x801e7a78` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC007.EMI: 0001627c` in the files
	* `0x801e7a7c` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC007.EMI: 00016280` in the files
	* `0x801e7a80` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
