Typhoon is a level 3 wind spell. It deals damage to everyone in the enemy party.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC006.EMI: 000157e8` in the files
	* `0x801e7fe8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC006.EMI: 000157ec` in the files
	* `0x801e7fec` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC006.EMI: 000157f0` in the files
	* `0x801e7ff0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
