Sever is a level 1 wind spell. It deals wind damage to a single target.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC004.EMI: 000130c4` in the files
	* `0x801e80c4` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC004.EMI: 000130c8` in the files
	* `0x801e80c8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC004.EMI: 000130cc` in the files
	* `0x801e80cc` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
