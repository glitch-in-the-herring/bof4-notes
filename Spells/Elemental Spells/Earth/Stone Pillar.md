Stone Pillar is a level 2 earth spell. It deals earth damage to a single target
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC011.EMI: 00018294` in the files
	* `0x801e9a94` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC011.EMI: 00018298` in the files
	* `0x801e9a98` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC011.EMI: 0001829c` in the files
	* `0x801e9a9c` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
