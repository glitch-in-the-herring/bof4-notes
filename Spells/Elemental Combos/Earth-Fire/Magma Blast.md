Magma Blast is the level 2 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by an earth and a level 2 fire spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC026.EMI: 00026b60` in the files
	* `0x801e7b60` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC026.EMI: 00026b64` in the files
	* `0x801e7b64` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC026.EMI: 00026b68` in the files
	* `0x801e7b68` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
