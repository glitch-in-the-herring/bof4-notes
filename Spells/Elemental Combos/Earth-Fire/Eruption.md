Eruption is the level 1 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by an earth and a level 1 fire spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC025.EMI: 00015fac` in the files
	* `0x801e97ac` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC025.EMI: 00015fb0` in the files
	* `0x801e97b0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC025.EMI: 00015fb4` in the files
	* `0x801e97b4` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
