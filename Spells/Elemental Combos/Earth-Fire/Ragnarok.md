Ragnarok is the level 3 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by an earth and a level 3 fire spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC027.EMI: 00024cd8` in the files
	* `0x801e9cd8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC027.EMI: 00024cdc` in the files
	* `0x801e9cdc` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC027.EMI: 00024ce0` in the files
	* `0x801e9ce0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
