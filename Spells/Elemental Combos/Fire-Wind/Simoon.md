Simoon is the level 2 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a fire and a level 2 wind spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC014.EMI: 00002b94` in the files
	* `0x801e8b94` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC014.EMI: 00002b98` in the files
	* `0x801e8b98` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC014.EMI: 00002b9c` in the files
	* `0x801e8b9c` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
