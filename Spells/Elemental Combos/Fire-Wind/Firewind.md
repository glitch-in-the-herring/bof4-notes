Firewind is the level 1 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a fire and a level 1 wind spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC013.EMI: 000142c8` in the files
	* `0x801e7ac8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC013.EMI: 000142cc` in the files
	* `0x801e7acc` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC013.EMI: 000142d0` in the files
	* `0x801e7ad0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
