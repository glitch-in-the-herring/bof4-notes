Gigaflare is the level 3 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a fire and a level 3 wind spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC015.EMI: 000167b4` in the files
	* `0x801e87b4` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC015.EMI: 000167b8` in the files
	* `0x801e87b8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC015.EMI: 000167bc` in the files
	* `0x801e87bc` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
