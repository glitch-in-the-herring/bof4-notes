Thunderstorm is the level 3 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a wind and a level 3 water spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC019.EMI: 00015bd0` in the files
	* `0x801e83d0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC019.EMI: 00015bd4` in the files
	* `0x801e83d4` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC019.EMI: 00015bd8` in the files
	* `0x801e83d8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
