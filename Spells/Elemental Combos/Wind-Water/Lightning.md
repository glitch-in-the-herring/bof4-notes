Lightning is the level 2 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a wind and a level 2 water spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC018.EMI: 0001f858` in the files
	* `0x801e8058` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC018.EMI: 0001f85c` in the files
	* `0x801e805c` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC018.EMI: 0001f860` in the files
	* `0x801e8060` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
