Jolt is the level 1 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a wind and a level 1 water spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC017.EMI: 00022460` in the files
	* `0x801e7c60` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC017.EMI: 00022464` in the files
	* `0x801e7c64` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC017.EMI: 00022468` in the files
	* `0x801e7c68` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
