Mjollnir is the [[Elemental Combos#Dragon Spell Combos|dragon spell combo]] formed by a wind spell, a water spell and the Pa Bing'ah spell, in that order.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC020.EMI: 000162d8` in the files
	* `0x801e92d8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC020.EMI: 000162dc` in the files
	* `0x801e92dc` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC020.EMI: 000162e0` in the files
	* `0x801e92e0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
