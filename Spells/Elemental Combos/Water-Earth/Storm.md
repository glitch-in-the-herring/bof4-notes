Storm is the level 1 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a water and a level 1 earth spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC021.EMI: 000155b0` in the files
	* `0x801e7db0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC021.EMI: 000155b4` in the files
	* `0x801e7db4` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC021.EMI: 000155b8` in the files
	* `0x801e7db8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
