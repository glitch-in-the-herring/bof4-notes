Tempest is the level 2 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a water and a level 2 earth spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC022.EMI: 0001682c` in the files
	* `0x801e802c` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC022.EMI: 00016830` in the files
	* `0x801e8030` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC022.EMI: 00016834` in the files
	* `0x801e8034` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
