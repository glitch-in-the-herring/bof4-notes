Disaster is the level 3 [[Elemental Combos|elemental combo]] formed by a water and a level 3 earth spell.
* As a standalone spell, its attack data can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC023.EMI: 00016ea8` in the files
	* `0x801e8ea8` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 100
* The damage percentage of the first sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC023.EMI: 00016eac` in the files
	* `0x801e8eac` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
* The damage percentage of the second sequela can be found at
	* `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC023.EMI: 00016eb0` in the files
	* `0x801e8eb0` in the RAM
	* Normally this is 25
