Palliate is a third-tier healing spell that you can only learn from Lampkins. They cast the spell after being given a [[Panacea]], then a [[Croc Tear]]. In addition to healing, it removes all status effects from the target. Its sequela is more healing.

- As a standalone spell, the heal data can be found at:
    - `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC117.EMI 0x00017108` in the files
    - Normally the power is 120 and the multiplier is 100
- The first sequela's heal data can be found at:
    - `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC117.EMI 0x0001710c`in the files
    - Normally the power is 120 and the multiplier is 110
- The second sequela's heal data can be found at
    - `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC117.EMI 0x00017110` in the files
    - Normally the power is 120 and the multiplier is 130
- The third sequela's heal data can be found at
    - `BIN/BMAGIC/MAGIC117.EMI 0x00017114` in the files
    - Normally the power is 120 and the multiplier is 25
