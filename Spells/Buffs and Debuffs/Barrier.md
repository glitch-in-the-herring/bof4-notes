Barrier reduces the amount of magic taken by half. *Magic*, as in *all magic*, not just magical damage. Healing spells, buffs, debuffs are all half as effective as well.

## Reduction
The following code can be found at `BIN/BATTLE/BTLMOVE.EMI: 00011ff0`
```
801d9ff0 lw      $v0, 0x000c(a0) ; load the damage calculated from the magic formula
801d9ff4 nop     
801d9ff8 srl     $v1, $v0, 0x1f
801d9ffc addu    $v0, $v1
801da000 sra     $v0, 0x01 ; divide the damage by 2
801da004 sw      $v0, 0x000c(a0) ; store the damage back
```
