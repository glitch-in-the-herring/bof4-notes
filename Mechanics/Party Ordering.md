The order of the party can be adjusted at the following locations:
Field order:
* `0x8011ad88` in the RAM. Six bytes
* ... in the save file
Battle order:
* `0x8011b6b4` in the RAM. Six bytes
* ...
Party members:
- `0x00`: Ryu
- `0x01`: Nina
- `0x02`: Cray
- `0x03`: Scias
- `0x04`: Ursula
- `0x05`: Ershin
- `0x06`: Fou-Lu
- `0xff`: Blank slot
Fou-Lu cannot be used in battles with anyone else.
