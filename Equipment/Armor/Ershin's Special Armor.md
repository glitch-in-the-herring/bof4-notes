Ershin has 21 special armor that can cast spells without the intervention of the player, and without consuming Ershin's turn. 

## Defender
Defender casts [[Barrier]] at the start of the player's turn. This can be changed at:
* `BIN/BATTLE/BTLMOVE.EMI: 00005b5c` in the file
* `0x801cdb5c` in the RAM
## Quicksilver
Quicksilver casts [[Speed]] at the start of the player's turn. This can be changed at:
* `BIN/BATTLE/BTLMOVE.EMI: 00005b6c` in the file
* `0x801cdb6c` in the RAM
